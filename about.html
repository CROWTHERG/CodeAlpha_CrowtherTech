About Me

Developer by Skill.
Designer by Eye.
Builder at Heart.

I'm Hammed Daniel Ajayi, a full-stack developer and Software Engineer operating under the brand CrowtherTech, based in Lagos, Nigeria. With over 5 years of hands-on experience, I specialise in building clean, functional, and visually compelling digital products for businesses and individuals around the world.

My work spans everything from university portals and admin dashboards to e-commerce platforms and REST APIs. I take pride in writing maintainable code and designing interfaces that people actually enjoy using — not just technically correct, but genuinely well-crafted.

Beyond the web, I write game scripts in GDScript for Godot Engine and Luau for Roblox Studio, building interactive gameplay mechanics and systems from scratch.

I believe great software lives at the intersection of technical precision and thoughtful design. Whether you're a startup, an SME, or an individual with a vision — I'll build it right, on time, and at a price that works.
